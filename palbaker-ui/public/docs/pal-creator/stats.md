# Editing Stats & Work Suitabilities

Once you have instantiated a custom Pal, you can fully customize its base combat stats, overworld size scales, and base camp work suitabilities.

## Combat Attributes & Size Tuning

- **Combat Stats**: Use the sliders in the creator card to modify HP, Melee Attack, Shot Attack, Defense, and Work Speed.
- **Primary & Secondary Element**: Set elements (such as Grass, Dark, or Dragon) using the dropdown menus.
- **Physical Scale**: Adjust the collision capsule radius and height to match your customized 3D model proportions, preventing your Pal from clipping through camp structures or sinking into the ground.

---

## Camp Work Suitabilities

Under the Work Suitabilities panel, check the boxes for any camp activities you want your Pal to participate in (e.g., Kindling, watering, planting, or mining). 

Checking a suitability box registers your standalone Pal with that capability, allowing camp controllers to assign them tasks natively when summoned at your base!
