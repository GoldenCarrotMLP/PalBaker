# Customizing Level-Up Learnsets

Every Pal species in Palworld has a "Learnset Matrix"—a table that dictates exactly what active combat moves they learn as they level up in your party. PalBaker allows you to fully customize this matrix for your standalone species.

## Modifying the Learnset Matrix

1. Expand your custom Pal's creator card and scroll to the **Learnset Matrix** panel.
2. **Add a Move**: Click the **Add Move** button, select from any active attack move in the game cache (such as *Sand Blast* or *Dragon Meteor*), and set the level requirement.
3. **Edit Level**: Change the number in the level column for any existing move.
4. **Change Move**: Click on the move name string to open the search list and swap the attack.
5. **Delete Move**: Click the red trash icon on the right of the row to remove the learnset entry.

## Database Synchronization
All available active attacks, their corresponding element classes, and baseline power metrics are dynamically cached from your game's raw pak archives, ensuring your customized movesets are 100% compliant with the active game patch.
